About Kofi Odenchem

Ebenezer Sakyi known by the stage name Kofi Odenchem was born in Breman Amoanda in the Central Region of Ghana and raised in Korle Gonno a residential town in Greater Accra Region. He attended primary school at President Barack Obama Basic School. And now in the Secondary school at Otumfuo Osei Tutu II College His songs became popular amd trending online when he released his massive hit in 2023 titled Sore.
